USHED HOMES

Welcome to Hushed Homes! This website is a tool to help you locate possible causes of noise in a specific location. Whether you're looking to buy, rent, or stay at a home, this simple tool will hopefully help you choose the most serene destination possible.

The pointers on the map are eiter nearby Airports or Heliports
The red lines show the path of airplanes when approaching or taking off from runways
Click on "Possible Disturbances" in order to find out more about aero-pollution!
